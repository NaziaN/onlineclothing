The Red Carpet Look

Perfect red carpet look

Nothing says icon like a signature red lipâ€”think Marilyn Monroe, Madonna, Gwen Stefani. It exudes a sense of power and sexiness at the same time and will always be in vogue. But you don't have to be a superstar to pull off the crimson pout. A bold pop of red will up the sex appeal of anyone's look. Today, to celebrate National Lipstick Day we're teaching you how to create the perfect, classic red lip with the help of CK One Color global makeup artist Kayleen McAdams. It may seem simple, but there is an art to achieving a sexy lip. The key is making sure you apply your products in the right order. Here, the three essential steps to follow:
STEP ONE: Choose your favorite red lipstick and use a brush to apply it to your lips starting on the bottom lip and applying the color from the middle out. Then, do the same on your top lip. This part doesn't have to be perfect, as we will fill in the line after. Just spread the lipstick in the general shape of the mouth.

STEP TWO: The trick to a perfectly defined and plump lip is applying the liner after the lipstick. This will give the liner some slipperiness and make it easier to accurately line your lips. Take a matching red liner (McAdams is using CK One Soft Defining Lip Pencil in Maven) and make sure it's completely sharpened. Apply it around the edges of your lips and be sure to emphasize your cupid's bow. If you have thinner lips, you can cheat for fullness by applying the liner just outside your lip line.
STEP THREE: Mush your lips together to press in the product. This will smooth things out and make the lipstick last longer.
