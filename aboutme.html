Hello there, I’m Nazia. I’ve very, very recently just moved back to Leicester after not living here for almost four years – spending three years studying Fine Art and then one year living in Central London, I’ve finally decided to head back. I’ve been dwelling on the idea of a space where I can curate my images and ramble to myself for what feels like forever now, and after months of planning my site is finally ready to show you all!. If you are a lover of food, travel, art, and most importantly people, I would love for you to join me on this journey. Posts will be going up every Wednesday and Sunday at 5pm! I have no idea what’s next…but I’m excited.
